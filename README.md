# Privacy Policy for Fallz

Last updated: March 17, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Fallz ("we", "us", or "our") handles information in our mobile application Fallz ("the App").

## Information We Collect

We do not collect personally identifiable information. The App collects:
- A nickname chosen by the user for leaderboard display purposes
- An anonymous device identifier to associate scores with devices
- Advertising data collected by Google AdMob for displaying ads

## How We Use Information

- Nicknames and device identifiers are used solely to display leaderboard scores
- Advertising data is used by Google AdMob to serve relevant advertisements

## Third Party Services

The App uses Google AdMob for advertising. Google's privacy policy is available at: https://policies.google.com/privacy

## Data Retention

Leaderboard scores and nicknames are stored in Firebase Realtime Database and may be retained indefinitely.
